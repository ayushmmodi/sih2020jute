About US

OUR PORTAL PROVIDES GENERAL INFORMATION ABOUT JUTE AND HOW ONE CAN IMPROVE JUTE CULTIVATION. WE WILL PROVIDE AN EMPLOYEE (GOVERNMENT/ORGANIZATIONAL) WHO WILL CONDUCT SURVEY AND WILL REGISTER DETAILS OF FARMERS AND MILL WORKERS (PERSONAL/INCOME) AND INCOME ANALYSIS WILL BE CONDUCTED ACCORDING TO THAT INFORMATION . IN THIS PORTAL, DATA AND STATE PAGE PROVIDES LIST OF MILLS AND INCOME ANALYSIS GRAPH (YEARLY/MONTHLY) .ALSO WE WILL PROVIDE ONLINE TUTORIALS FOR THE BETTER UNDERSTANDING OF FARMERS AND MILL WORKERS. AS WE ALL KNOW LINGUISTIC PROBLEMS ARE ALWAYS A BARRIER SO WE PROVIDE OPTION TO CHOOSE LANGUAGE ACCORDING TO THEIR CONVINIENCE . THERE ARE MULTIPLE FACILITES SUCH AS PRODUCT LIST, WEATHER FORCASTING, INCOME ASSESSMENT TABLE, STATUTE(ACTS,ORDERS, POLICIES,SCHEMES) WHICH ARE AVAILABLE IN OUR PORTAL..
